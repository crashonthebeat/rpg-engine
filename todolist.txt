DOCUMENTATION OF ALL THINGS


ITEM SLOTS:
    Getting or buying an item puts it in your hands, which have a weight and item limit.
    In order to free your hands, the item needs to be placed into a valid slot, or into a container (backpack, pouch, etc.)
    Items have sizes, and containers have size limits. 

ITEM DESCRIPTIONS:
    Items should have descriptions based on where they are in the room.

ROOM DESCRIPTIONS:
    Two kinds of room inventories: open and closed
    Open inventories have their inventories added to the room.
        IE - Tables, racks, shelves, etc.
    Closed inventories must be taken from directly.
        IE - Chests, armoires, backpacks.
